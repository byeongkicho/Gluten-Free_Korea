# Celiac Dining Card (Korean): Printable Phrases

This page is designed to be shown to staff. Keep it short, polite, and clear.

This is not medical advice. If you have severe allergy/celiac, prioritize safety.

## Short version (most useful)

Copy/paste this block into your phone notes.

**Korean**

-    . ()
-  /  /    .
- ()  .
-       ?

**English meaning**

- I have celiac disease (gluten sensitivity).
- I cannot eat wheat/barley/rye.
- Soy sauce often contains wheat. Please check ingredients.
- Please use clean utensils/pan and avoid cross-contamination.

## Strict version (for very sensitive celiac)

Use when you need to emphasize cross-contamination.

**Korean**

-    .
- ()   .
-   ? ()
-  ? ()

**English meaning**

- Even a small amount of gluten makes me sick.
- Please use clean utensils and a clean pan.
- Is the grill shared?
- Is the fryer shared?

## Restaurant types to be careful with

High-risk by default:

- BBQ with shared grills
- Fried chicken / tempura (shared fryers)
- Noodle shops (shared boiling water)
- Buffets (shared utensils)
